Architecture

Five Layers. One Intelligent System.

Every task flows through Murphy's dynamic pipeline — soul-loaded agents, business logic gates, resolution scaling, and swarm execution.

Layer 1 — Soul
Agent Identity
Each agent loads its Rosetta soul document — who it is, what it values, and what its authority boundaries are. Your CEO is always the same person.
Layer 2 — Rosetta
Org Dispatch
The live org chart assigns roles dynamically. Collector, Translator, Executor, Scheduler, and Auditor agents receive tasks matched to their soul.
Layer 3 — MFGC
Business Logic Gates
7-phase control gates ensure every task is legal, safe, complete, and authorized before execution. Gates self-evaluate against live system data.
Layer 4 — MSS
Resolution Scaling
Tasks are magnified or simplified based on complexity and authority scope. MSS ensures every agent gets work sized to its capability.
Layer 5 — Swarm
Execution
The swarm executes in parallel DAG order. Every step emits a notification. Work items persist. Results flow to your CEO console in plain English.
