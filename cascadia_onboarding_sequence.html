Decision & Owner

Decision: Treat onboarding as a 90-day pipeline, not a Day-1 event. Pre-stage Phase A artifacts 5 business days before start. Bake SST card enrollment into the offer letter as a hire-day prerequisite (not a job-day prerequisite). Owner: HR Director.

Open risk. Missing Phase D field credentials (SST, OSHA 30) means the new hire cannot productively bill NYC hours past Day 30. At 200-employee scale with 25-40 hires/yr, that's 750-1,200 unbillable ramp-hours if uncontrolled.
