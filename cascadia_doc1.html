Question A1 — Cross-State Licensing

Oregon CCB License & New York Work — Reciprocity Reality

"Can my Oregon CCB license be used to do MEP work in New York, or do I need separate NY registration?"

Short Answer

No. Oregon's Construction Contractors Board (CCB) license does not confer New York contracting privileges. New York does not have a general reciprocity agreement with Oregon for construction contractor licensure. Cascadia must register and license at multiple levels: (1) NY Department of State foreign entity authority, (2) NYC Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (DCWP) for any work within the five boroughs, and (3) per-trade licensure for any individual on staff performing master-class work NYC Admin. Code §20-385 et seq. 19 NYCRR §1300.

The Registration Path (Sequenced)

  1. NY Department of State — Foreign Entity Authority. File Application for Authority (Form DOS-1336-f for LLCs). Requires a Certificate of Good Standing from Oregon Secretary of State dated within 60 days. Filing fee $250 (LLC). Designate NY DOS as agent for process.
  2. EIN review & NY State Tax registration. Register for NY corporate franchise tax (even as a foreign LLC, a NY job creates nexus) and withholding tax through NY Online Services for Business.
  3. NYC DCWP — Home Improvement Contractor / General Contractor. File for HIC license if any residential work; for commercial-only MEP, confirm Tracking Number assignment via DOB NOW: Build. License fee $100, 2-year term. Background check required.
  4. Per-trade master licenses. For each NYC plumbing or electrical scope, a NYC-licensed Master Plumber or Master Electrician must be the licensee of record on the permit. Cascadia options: (a) hire a NYC-licensed master, (b) partner with a NYC trade contractor of record, or (c) have the NY job filed under the GC's master licensee with Cascadia as named subcontractor.
  5. Workers Compensation. Oregon SAIF coverage does not satisfy NY. Procure a NY-domiciled WC policy or extension (NY State Insurance Fund or licensed admitted carrier), plus NY DBL (Disability Benefits Law) and PFL (Paid Family Leave) coverage for any employee working a NY worksite.

What Cascadia's CCB License DOES Carry Across

Reputation evidence only. Your Oregon CCB record (bond claims, complaints, license history) is admissible when a NYC DCWP background check is run. A clean CCB history accelerates DCWP issuance but does not substitute for it.
