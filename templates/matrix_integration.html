HITL Approvals

Pending human-in-the-loop interventions posted to the Matrix hitl-approvals room. Approve or reject directly from here.

Escalations

HITL interventions that have timed out and been escalated to the murphy-escalations room.

Escalations are posted automatically when a HITL intervention exceeds the configured timeout (default: 60 minutes). Check the murphy-escalations Matrix room for details.

Configuration

Poll IntervalHITL_POLL_INTERVAL env var (default: 30s)
Approval TimeoutHITL_APPROVAL_TIMEOUT env var (default: 3600s)
Escalation RoomHITL_ESCALATION_ROOM env var (default: murphy-escalations)
