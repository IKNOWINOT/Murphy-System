Your Personal AI

Shadow Agents:
Your Digital Twin

A Shadow Agent is your shadow — it represents your personal IP and learns how to do things based on how you do them. It watches, learns, and gradually takes over routine work while preserving your unique style and expertise.

  • Learns your personal workflow patterns
  • Preserves your unique style & decision-making
  • Your IP stays yours — never shared
  • Graduates from shadow to autonomous over time
Get Your Shadow Agent
$ murphy shadow status
👁 Observing your workflow…
—————————
$ murphy shadow learn --from-you
✓ Learned: proposal formatting
✓ Learned: review style
—————————
$ murphy shadow draft proposal
✓ Draft matches your style 94%
—————————
$ murphy shadow graduate --task=intake
