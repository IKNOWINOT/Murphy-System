4. Scope of Automation

Murphy System, operated by Inoni Limited Liability Company, provides AI-powered business generation, document creation, data analysis, and workflow automation services. Murphy generates; it does not physically execute regulated or physical-world activities. Any such activities require a qualified human professional as described in this Agreement.

By activating any automated operation through Murphy System, the authorising operator ("the Approver") acknowledges that:

  1. they have reviewed the generated output and approve its deployment;
  2. they accept responsibility for outcomes arising from their approval;
  3. the Human-in-the-Loop ("HITL") approval is binding and constitutes the Approver's explicit acceptance of the financial and operational risk described in this Agreement.

Inoni Limited Liability Company's liability is strictly limited to the scope of Murphy System's generation and automation services. Inoni is not liable for:

  3. the accuracy of regulatory information generated by Murphy (human review required);
  4. any loss arising after the Approver's HITL acceptance.

7. Risk Transfer Upon HITL Approval

Upon approval of any Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) request, ALL operational and financial risk associated with the approved deployment is transferred to the Approver. This transfer is irrevocable for the approved operation. The Approver warrants that they have the authority and expertise to approve the described activity, and that they accept personal and professional responsibility for its outcome.

9. Contractor Sole Responsibility

Any contractor dispatched through the system to perform physical-world tasks (inspection, notarization, filing, calibration, etc.) is an independent professional solely responsible for their licensed work. The contractor's acceptance of a task constitutes their agreement to perform the work in accordance with their professional licence and the applicable regulatory body's standards. Inoni LLC is not liable for any errors, omissions, or professional failures by dispatched contractors.

10. Credentialed Human-in-the-Loop Validation

Activities requiring a certification, licence, or regulatory body oversight must be validated by a human professional holding the relevant credential. As part of their HITL acceptance, the validating professional provides their credential information (licence number, issuing body, expiry date) which is recorded in the system. By providing this information and accepting the task, the professional:

  1. warrants that their credential is current and valid;
  2. accepts full professional responsibility for the regulated activity;
  3. agrees that any failure in their professional duty is their sole liability and not that of Inoni LLC or Murphy System.

11. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising from a niche business operation shall first be referred to the HITL approver who authorised the relevant operation, as the recorded risk-accepting party. Inoni LLC is not the primary respondent in disputes arising from approved automations. All HITL approvals, COYA records, and negotiation documents are maintained as the definitive record of what was agreed, approved, and executed.

If a dispute cannot be resolved with the HITL approver, the parties agree to mediation and, failing that, binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association (AAA).
