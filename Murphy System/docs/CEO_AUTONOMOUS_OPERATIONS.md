## Self-Advertising and Outreach Plan

### Core Principle: The Outreach IS the Demo

Every cold-outreach message sent by `MurphySelfSellingEngine` is itself a live
demonstration of Murphy's capabilities:

- **Real-time stats injected** — actual `emails_sent`, `state_changes`,
  `projects_active` counters appear in the message body.
- **Business-specific constraint generation** — the email shows the prospect
  their *own* automation constraints, generated by `BUSINESS_TYPE_CONSTRAINTS`
  for their industry vertical.
- **3-day free trial offer** — the CTA leads directly to automated trial
  provisioning via `TrialOrchestrator` and `TrialShadowDeployer`.
- **META_PROOF message** — the fact that the message was composed and sent by
  Murphy is *itself* the product demo.

### Targeting

Murphy currently supports 12 industry verticals via `BUSINESS_TYPE_CONSTRAINTS`
(restaurant, law, medical, real estate, logistics, education, SaaS, ecommerce,
trades/construction, consulting, accounting, marketing agencies). Each vertical
has pre-computed automation constraints and pain-point templates.

### Channel Strategy

| Channel | Role | Daily Cap | Compliance Rule |
|---------|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Email** | Primary outreach | 50/day | CAN-SPAM: unsubscribe link + physical address required |
| **LinkedIn** | Secondary outreach | 30/day | Platform TOS rate limits enforced |
| **SMS** | Tertiary (consent-only) | 20/day | TCPA: prior express consent required |

Caps are enforced by `OutreachComplianceGovernor` (SSE-COMPL-001) and are
configurable via its `daily_limits` constructor parameter.

### Contact Cadence

| Scenario | Cadence |
|----------|---------|
| Cold prospect (non-customer) | Max 1 contact per channel per 30 days |
| Existing customer (marketing) | Max 1 contact per channel per 7 days |
| Active trial / service message | No cooldown (transactional exemption) |
| Opted-out prospect | **Permanently suppressed** — no contact until human override |

The 20-minute selling cycle in `run_selling_cycle()` runs continuously. Each
cycle discovers new prospects, checks compliance for every candidate, and only
sends to those who pass all gates.

### Compliance Rules (Embedded in Code)

All rules are enforced at the `OutreachComplianceGovernor` layer — not just
policy, but executable code that cannot be bypassed:

| Regulation | Rule Enforced |
|-----------|--------------|
| **CAN-SPAM** | Unsubscribe mechanism + physical address in every email |
| **TCPA** | No SMS/calls without prior express consent |
| **GDPR Art. 21** | Opt-out recorded permanently; data subject rights honoured |
| **CASL** | Express consent required for Canadian prospects |
| **PECR** | Suppression list maintained with full audit trail |

### Community Building

- Trial graduates become **case studies** (metrics reports sent on day 4).
- Shadow agents deployed during trials remain active after conversion, creating
  stickiness and reference data.
- Metrics reports become **social proof** for future outreach.
- `ContractorAugmentedIntel` dispatches researchers to fill intelligence gaps
  that Murphy cannot gather autonomously.

## Generalised Workflow for Self-Sufficiency

The 10-step autonomous operations loop:

```
Step 1 — DISCOVER
  Module: SalesAutomationEngine.generate_leads()
  Input:  BUSINESS_TYPE_CONSTRAINTS (12 verticals)
  Output: List[ProspectProfile]

Step 2 — QUALIFY
  Module: ProspectOnboarder.onboard()
  Input:  Raw lead data
  Output: Qualified ProspectProfile with automation constraints

Step 3 — COMPLY-CHECK
  Module: OutreachComplianceGovernor.check_contact_allowed()
  Input:  prospect_id, channel
  Output: ComplianceDecision (ALLOWED | BLOCKED_*)
  Gate:   Hard block on opt-out; 30-day cooldown; daily rate limit

Step 4 — OUTREACH
  Module: SelfSellingOutreach.send() via MurphySelfSellingEngine
  Input:  Compliant ProspectProfile
  Output: OutreachMessage sent; contact recorded for cooldown

Step 5 — MONITOR REPLIES
  Module: MurphySelfSellingEngine.handle_prospect_reply()
  Input:  Reply text
  Output: "trial_started" | "nurture" | opt-out → permanent suppression

Step 6 — TRIAL
  Module: TrialOrchestrator.start_trial()
  Input:  ProspectProfile + business_context
  Output: 3-day trial provisioned; TrialShadowDeployer active

Step 7 — CONVERT
  Module: SubscriptionManager + BillingAPI (PayPal / Coinbase)
  Input:  Trial completion signal
  Output: Paid subscription activated; prospect marked as customer

Step 8 — PRODUCE
  Module: ProductionAssistantEngine (PROD-001)
  Input:  ProductionProposal + ProductionWorkOrder
  Output: Validated deliverable at ≥ 0.99 confidence; lifecycle tracked

Step 9 — REPORT
  Module: FinancialReportingEngine + TrialMetricsReport
  Input:  Active profiles + billing events
  Output: Automated metrics report; case study data

Step 10 — LEARN
  Module: LibrarianEngine + GoldenPathBridge
  Input:  All cycle outcomes
  Output: Updated knowledge graph; improved future outreach personalisation
```

## Escalation Paths

Murphy operates autonomously within defined boundaries. When a boundary is
crossed, the escalation path is:

1. **Gate block** (e.g., compliance block, confidence < 0.99) → logged,
   skipped automatically.
2. **HITL gate** (human-in-the-loop required for certified work) →
   `HITLBridge` routes to human approver queue.
3. **Hard error** (unhandled exception in core loop) → `AlertRulesEngine`
   fires alert; error logged to audit trail.
4. **Human override** (e.g., clearing an opt-out) → requires `cleared_by`
   and `audit_reason` — logged permanently.
