Swarm heartbeat is implemented in src/swarm_scheduler.py. It runs every 5 minutes, sending a status signal to each of the 9 agents: collector, translator, scheduler, executor, auditor, exec_admin, prod_ops, hitl, and rosetta. This ensures every agent runs, the soul gate fires, and runs_total increments for all. The job is registered with id 'swarm_heartbeat' and name 'Swarm-wide heartbeat'. Despite the interval being set to 5 minutes in code, the comment in the jobs dictionary incorrectly states '30min', which may be a documentation error.
