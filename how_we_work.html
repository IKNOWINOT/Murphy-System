Common questions

What if I'm a small owner-operator?
We have a $500/month flat tier for businesses under 2 employees. That covers reception, sales follow-up, and basic bookkeeping — all with HITL approvals so you stay in control.
How are savings verified?
We baseline your pre-engagement costs in writing during discovery. Each month we compare actuals to that baseline. You get the methodology and the receipts. No black box.
What if Murphy doesn't save me anything?
Then you don't pay the performance fee. You only paid the T&M setup cost (fixed, scoped upfront). The automations we built are still yours to keep.
Do I have to host this myself?
No. Murphy runs on your existing server, our cloud, or a dedicated Hetzner node. Your data never leaves your environment unless you want it to.
