## Strategy

Murphy System is raising **$500K on a SAFE at a $5M–$7M valuation cap** — no discount, no board seats. The strategy is to secure 2–3 high-conviction angels whose networks span the three verticals Murphy monetises: industrial OT, enterprise/business automation, and content creator workflows. All materials below are designed to let investors evaluate Murphy in under 10 minutes.
