How It Works

Tell Murphy your idea. Go do something else.

Murphy spins up a full business stack in minutes. No team. No meetings. No overhead. Just a swarm of agents executing around the clock.

Step 01
💡

Bring Your Idea

Describe your business in plain language — or paste a URL. Murphy's intake swarm researches the market, identifies competitors, and builds your initial strategy document in minutes.

Step 02
🏗️

Murphy Builds the Stack

Engineering agents spin up your server, deploy your product, configure DNS, set up your payment processor, CRM, email infrastructure, and analytics — all without asking questions.

Step 03
📡

Agents Run Operations

Every night a CEO agent runs a business review cycle: What grew? What broke? What should we build next? It dispatches tasks to Marketing, Engineering, Support, and Finance agents automatically.

Step 04
✍️

You Provide the 20%

Murphy handles 80% autonomously. The 20% that needs a human — big strategic calls, signing contracts, approving capital allocation — Murphy surfaces them via HITL. You decide. Murphy executes.
