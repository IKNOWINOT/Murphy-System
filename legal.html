As part of their HITL acceptance, the validating professional provides their credential information (licence number, issuing body, expiry date) which is recorded in the system.   1. warrants that their credential is current and valid;

  3. agrees that any failure in their professional duty is their sole liability and not that of Murphy Collective or Murphy System.

14. Shadow Agent Constructs & Five-Year Forfeiture

Plain English: Murphy Collective licenses the Murphy platform to you. Your data inputs and outputs are yours — always. The system engine is ours. Shadow automation constructs (workflow configurations) are licensed to you while you use them. Build something and never deploy it within 5 years? The construct forfeits to Murphy Collective. Your actual data is never affected.

14.1 Shadow Automation Constructs — Licensed to You

Workflow configurations and automation templates you build using the shadow agent ("Shadow Automation Constructs") are system artifacts created on top of Murphy Collective's licensed platform. You are granted a licence to use, operate, and modify your Shadow Automation Constructs for as long as your account is active and the construct is deployed in a production workflow. The underlying system technology that powers those constructs remains Murphy Collective's exclusive property at all times.

14.2 Forfeiture of Unmade Constructs

Any Shadow Automation Construct that has not been deployed or activated in a production workflow within five (5) years of its creation date ("Unmade Construct") is automatically and irrevocably forfeited to Murphy Collective upon expiry of that five-year period, without any further notice or compensation. Upon forfeiture, the licence for that construct terminates and Murphy Collective may use, develop, or commercialise the Unmade Construct without restriction.

User data is not affected: Input Data and Output Data that flowed through any previously active constructs remain your property and are not subject to this forfeiture provision.

14.3 Corporate and Org-Chart Automation Constructs

Automation constructs created within an organisation's workspace are licensed to that organisation and are subject to the same five-year forfeiture clause for Unmade Constructs under section 14.2 above. The organisation's underlying business data is not affected.
