FAQ

How do I pay? Do you take credit cards?

Right now Murphy bills through NOWPayments (crypto rail — BTC, ETH, USDC, others). Credit-card support is coming once we cross 500 users.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month or annual. Email cpost@murphy.systems to cancel — no questions, no retention dance.

How fast do you respond?

Urgent: within 4 hours. High: same business day. Normal: 1–2 days. Low: within a week.

Is my data private?

Yes. Tenants are isolated. We don't sell data. Per-tenant souls and data don't cross tenant boundaries.

What's Murphy actually built on?

Self-hosted infrastructure on dedicated hardware. NOWPayments for billing. No third-party trackers. Open about what we use — ask if you want specifics.
