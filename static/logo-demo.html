👁 How it works: Pure SVG. The iris and pupil are independent <g> elements with their own transform. JS computes the gaze vector from cursor → logo center, clamps with a tanh() saturation curve, and translates them in viewBox units. The pupil moves 1.55× further than the iris — matching real eye optics. Smooth, GPU-accelerated, infinite directions, scales perfectly.
