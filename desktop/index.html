Privacy-by-design · DLF-Lite wire format

Murphy Desktop Agent.
Your data stays on your machine.

Murphy runs locally on your computer, learns from how you actually work, and never sends your files, prompts, or screen contents to our server. What flows back is metacognition — not content.

What we see vs. what we don't

✓What we receive
• Which Rosetta persona was injected (e.g. "executor")
• Your DLF snapshot ID (north star + capacity)
• Which capability APIs were used
• Paired_score + fitness_delta (0..1 floats)
✗What we never see
• Your prompt text
• Your files, documents, or screen contents
• The URLs you visit
• The agent's output text

Built on Murphy's existing R105 pair_evaluation() + DLF-Lite (Shogun v0.1) wire format, extended desktop-ward.
