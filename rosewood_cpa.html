Operations Briefing

Running A CPA Firm — Universal Core + Accounting Overlay

A 12-partner Pacific Northwest CPA firm operates 80 universal-core forms + 25 accounting-specific artifacts.
Same pipeline, different tenant. Rosewood is NOT a contractor. Yet the same Murphy template engine — same universal-core spec, same form catalog generator — renders Rosewood's compliance footprint cleanly. Brand assets (logo mark, primary/secondary colors, typography) are the tenant's. The form scaffolding is platform-wide.
